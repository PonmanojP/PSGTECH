Why PSG

Welcome to PSG college of technology

PSG College of Technology, an ISO 9001:2015 certified institution is one of the foremost institutions founded by the PSG & Sons' Charities Trust(1926). The College was established in the year 1951 and the Founders wisely decided to locate it in the same campus as the PSG Indistrial Institute for effective industry-institute interaction.

Provide world-class Engineering Education, Foster Research and Development. Evolve innovative applications of Technology. Encourage Entrepreneurship. Ultimately mould young men and women capable of assuming leadership of the society for the betterment of the Country.
